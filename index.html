What we build

Security infrastructure for devices that cannot rely on heavy credential models.

Connected products increasingly handle health, payment, operational, and safety-critical data. Yet many wearables and IoT devices operate with limited compute, memory, and battery capacity, making conventional security stacks difficult to deploy without performance trade-offs.

GuardWeave Labs develops lightweight authentication and trust technologies that help organizations verify that every device is genuine, authorized, and suitable for trusted communication before sensitive data is exchanged.

Flagship product

PUFGuard: lightweight device authentication for OEMs and connected-device platforms.

PUFGuard is a security layer for authenticating resource-constrained devices using hardware-derived identity. It is designed to support OEM integration, device enrollment, secure verification, and trusted communication across connected-device ecosystems.

01

Verify genuine devices

PUFGuard helps organizations confirm that a device is authentic and difficult to clone before allowing trusted data exchange.

02

Reduce secret-extraction risk

The solution is designed around hardware-derived identity, limiting dependence on long-term secrets stored on constrained devices.

03

Support secure sessions

PUFGuard enables authenticated pairing and protected communication between devices, clients, and backend systems.

Why GuardWeave

Cybersecurity expertise translated into deployable device-trust technology.

GuardWeave Labs focuses on practical security for constrained devices: hardware-rooted identity, lightweight cryptography, and AI-assisted insights that help organizations assess trust across device fleets.

Hardware-rooted identity

Device identity is anchored in physical device behavior, making cloning and impersonation substantially harder than copying credentials alone.

AI-assisted trust intelligence

Security signals can be organized into actionable fleet-level insight, helping teams identify abnormal authentication patterns and deployment risk.

OEM-ready integration

The product direction supports factory provisioning, firmware integration, backend verification, and pilot deployment with device manufacturers.

Designed for constrained devices

PUFGuard is built for environments where security must fit within strict CPU, memory, and power limits.
